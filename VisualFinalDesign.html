Sketches

To initiate the foundational design of the app, we created sketches because they're an affordable and efficient method to convey our vision for the app's layout to one another.

Following some deliberation and fine-tuning of our initial sketches, we developed an improved design with a less cluttered UI, allowing users to easily switch between different social media platforms.


Wireframes for Social, News, and Fitness/Health Hub with Tabbed Navigation

Wireframes provide a foundational structure for designing the user interface of an application. Given the unique challenges faced by the elderly, our wireframes are rooted in our comprehensive research and survey results. Our focus is on crafting a central hub that facilitates social connections, delivers news updates, and manages fitness/health activities.

Main Tab Navigation:

Prominently placed at the top, the main tab list provides users with quick access to core functionalities:

  • Socials: Centralizes social media integrations and communications.
  • News: Offers tailored news updates based on user preferences.
  • Health & Fitness: A dedicated space for health management and fitness activities.
  • Settings: Allows users to configure the app, including adding more external links or platforms.

Socials Tab:

Upon selecting the Socials tab:

  • Users are greeted with a feed showcasing recent posts from integrated platforms, like Facebook.
  • An easy-to-use search bar allows users to locate friends, family, or specific communities.
  • A 'Chat' button directs users to a simplified messaging platform, streamlining their interactions.

News Tab:

Engaging the News tab provides:

  • A personalized news feed tailored to the user's chosen topics and interests.
  • Options to filter news by categories, such as Health, World, Local News, etc.
  • A user-friendly article display with an adjustable font size feature.
  • A 'Save for Later' function, so users can earmark articles they wish to come back to.

Health & Fitness Tab:

Clicking on the Health & Fitness tab may reveal applications such as:

  • A dashboard offering a snapshot of upcoming medical appointments.
  • Daily medication reminders.
  • A 'Progress' section that displays health metrics, such as blood pressure readings or daily steps.
  • Personalized fitness plans along with 'Daily Challenges' for light exercises.
  • Integrated stats from platforms like Google Fit, providing real-time tracking of fitness metrics.

Settings Tab:

Venturing into the Settings tab, users can:

  • Add or link other social media accounts.
  • Customize news preferences to receive more relevant updates.
  • Adjust health and fitness goals or integrate with other health platforms.
  • Add additional external links or platforms they wish to have accessible through the app, ensuring a tailored experience.

Conclusion:

With the inclusion of the main tab navigation, our wireframes ensure a smooth, intuitive experience for the elderly. The tabs are designed for clarity, allowing users to easily switch between essential functions. By prioritizing user-friendly design and ease of access, our goal is to create an app that caters to the unique needs of our target demographic while providing them with a comprehensive digital experience.


Mockups

Upon logging in, users are directed to the "Social Media" page, eliminating the need for a separate home page. Switching between different social media platforms is a breeze. Users simply hover over the "Social Media" tab and a dropdown menu presents a list of available platforms to select from.
Not only limited to social media, users can swiftly toggle between various feeds — be it "Social Media", "Health & Fitness", or "News" — just by tapping the desired field located at the top of the page. This streamlined design ensures a smooth and seamless navigation experience.
For those looking to personalize their experience, our app offers flexibility. If users wish to incorporate additional social media networks, fitness apps, or news websites, they can do so with ease. A trip to the "Settings" page provides an option to add the link of the desired platform, allowing users to truly make the app their own.
The primary aim of our app is to keep things straightforward. With its intuitive interface, even users who might find technology daunting, such as the elderly, can navigate with confidence.
