Conclusion

What We Learned

One of the most challenging aspects of crafting this application was gaining a deep understanding of the unique concerns and needs of our elderly audience. Recognizing the distinct apprehensions and potential technophobia of the older generation. Through consistent and patient communication with our target demographic, we dove deep into their concerns, aspirations, and hesitations. Our research allowed us to pinpoint the root causes of their reluctance towards using a mobile app, a sentiment strongly influenced by their life experiences and the rapidly changing technological landscape around them. With the invaluable insights we garnered, we meticulously shaped a mobile application tailored for the elderly. Our final product not only captures their interest but also fosters a sense of security.
